Through interactive maps, charts, and visual representations, we present a comprehensive analysis of the financial resources allocated to policing, highlighting areas where funds may be spent on practices that do not align with the best interests of the community. Our data-driven approach allows users to explore and understand how these resources could be more effectively redirected towards initiatives that promote public safety and well-being

As advocates for change, we offer evidence-based solutions and alternatives that have proven successful in various communities. Our platform showcases successful programs, community-based initiatives, and restorative justice models that have yielded positive outcomes and fostered stronger relationships between law enforcement and the communities they serve.

At "Rethinking Criminal Justice," we firmly believe that safety and justice are achievable without perpetuating oppressive systems. By reimagining policing and exploring alternative approaches, we aim to create a safer, more equitable, and inclusive society for everyone.
