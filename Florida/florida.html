"Abolition is about presence, not absence. It's about building life-affirming institutions."
- Ruth Wilson Gilmore
"With an annual budget of approximately $3.6 billion for policing, Florida's investment is disproportionately focused on punitive measures rather than community well-being. This financial misalignment hampers essential sectors like education, where only about 3% of the state budget is allocated. Alarmingly, the state's spending on policing is nearly three times higher than its spending on higher education. Tragically, from 2013 to 2022, 1,201 individuals lost their lives due to police encounters in Florida, as documented by Mapping Police Violence. It's a heartbreaking toll that underscores the urgent need to rethink public safety and allocate resources toward community well-being."
- Rethinking Criminal Justice
