Police have been abolished
"Rather, viral Justice requires each of us to demand that the money normally invested in policing be reinvested in what we actually need to sustain us: mental health services, better education, better healthcare, homes."
- Ruha Benjamin
"Evidence from Mapping Police Violence shows that each year more than 1000 people in the US lose their lives due to police violence. Besides that, the national survey by the Center for Democracy and Technology showed that 44% of students have been contacted by the police as a result of student monitoring, which in other words means that they might be the future victims of the police violence. Even though that is the case, Urban Institute shows that the police budget in the US surpasses $100 billion, which often overshadows investments in education, health care, and social services. Clearly we don't need to lose our people through the taxes we pay, this is the right time we ask to redirect these resorces towards community-driven initiatives that will cultivate safer and more equitable environments"
- Rethinking Criminal Justice
